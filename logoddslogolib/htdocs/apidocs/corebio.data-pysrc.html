 35  """ 
 36  Standard data used in computational biology. 
 37   
 38   
 39  To convert a property dictionary to a list : 
 40  >>> comp = [ amino_acid_composition[k] for k in amino_acid_letters] 
 41   
 42   
 43   
 44  Resources:  
 45      Various standard data files are included in the corebio distribution. These 
 46      may be loaded with the data_string, data_stream or data_filename methods. 
 47      A complete set of names is stored in 'resource_names' 
 48    
 49  BLOSUM Scoring Matrices 
 50      Source: ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/repository/blocks/unix/blosum 
 51      These are all new blast style with 1/3 bit scaling 
 52      - blosum35 
 53      - blosum45     
 54      - blosum62     
 55      - blosum40     
 56      - blosum50     
 57      - blosum80     
 58      - blosum100    
 59   
 60  Other substitution scoring matrices: 
 61      - dist20_comp  
 62      - pam250 
 63      - pam120 
 64      - vtml160 
 65       
 66  Description of database cross references : 
 67      - dbxref.txt (http://www.expasy.org/cgi-bin/lists?dbxref.txt) 
 68   
 69       
 70  Attributes: 
 71      - amino_acid_letters 
 72          -- Standard codes for the 20 canonical amino acids, in alphabetic 
 73          order. 
 74           
 75      - amino_acid_alternative_letters 
 76          -- Amino acid one letter codes, alphabetic by three letter codes. 
 77   
 78      - amino_acid_extended_letters 
 79   
 80      - dna_letters 
 81   
 82      - dna_extended_letters 
 83   
 84      - rna_letters 
 85       
 86      - rna_extended_letters 
 87   
 88      - dna_ambiguity  
 89   
 90      - rna_ambiguity 
 91       
 92      - amino_acid_ambiguity 
 93       
 94      - amino_acid_mass 
 95          -- Monomer isotopically averaged molecular mass  
 96       
 97      - dna_mass 
 98       
 99      - rna_mass 
100           
101      - one_to_three       
102          -- Map from standard 1 letter amino acid codes to standard three 
103          letter codes.  
104          Ref: http://www.ebi.ac.uk/RESID/faq.html 
105         
106      - standard_three_to_one 
107          -- Map from standard 3 letter amino acid codes to standard 1 
108          letter codes. 
109            
110      - extended_three_to_one 
111          -- Map between three letter amino acid codes (first letter capitalized)  
112          and standard one letter codes. This map contains many nonstandard three 
113          letter codes, used, for example, to specify chemically modified amino 
114          acids in PDB files. 
115          Ref: http://astral.berkeley.edu/  
116          Ref: http://www.ebi.ac.uk/RESID/faq.html 
117   
118      - amino_acid_names 
119   
120      - amino_acid_composition 
121          -- Average amino acid composition of proteins. 
122          Ref: McCaldon P., Argos P. Proteins 4:99-122 (1988). 
123   
124      - kyte_doolittle_hydrophobicity  
125          -- Kyte-Doolittle hydrophobicity scale. 
126          Ref: Kyte J., Doolittle R.F. J. Mol. Biol. 157:105-132 (1982) 
127           
128      - nucleotide_names 
129       
130      - amino_acid_accesible_surface_area 
131          -- Nominal maximum solvent accessoble area for unmodified amino acids, 
132          in square Angstroms. 
133          Ref: Sander & Rost, (1994), Proteins, 20:216-226 
134   
135   
136  Status: Beta (Data needs to be proof checked.)
